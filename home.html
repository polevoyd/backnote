About Backnote

Backnote is a company that makes your online notekeeping easier. It was founded in 2018 in Seattle by four Code Fellows students during project week. After many sleepless nights of coding, midnight runs for al pastor tacos and an occasional shot of Stolichnaya, Backnote was launched in August 2018. We devote all of our time and effort to making your browsing and notekeeping experience enjoyable and easy. With Backnote you will no longer have to switch between tabs during browsing sessions to make and store notes. Furthermore Backnote makes it very easy to find your notes back, store them and download it in various formats. Above all it's FREE! Keeping tabs on your browsing notes is a breeze with Backnote.
