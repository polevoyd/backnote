About Backnote

Backnote is a company that makes your online notekeeping easier. It was founded in 2018 in Seattle by four Code Fellows students during project week. After many sleepless nights of coding, midnight runs for al pastor tacos and an occasional shot of Stolichnaya, Backnote was launched in August 2018. We devote all our time and effort to making your browsing and notekeeping experience enjoyable and easy. With Backnote you will no longer have to switch between tabs during browsing sessions to make and store notes. Furthermore it's very easy to find your notes back in an ordered fashion on your personal notebook page. Keeping tabs on your browsing notes is a breeze with Backnote.

How does it work?

Working with Backnote is really easy. Follow these instructions and you're bound to be successful!

  • Go to https://addons.mozilla.org, search for Backnote and download the extension. Once downloaded the extension will appear in the top right corner of your browser bar.
  • Go to the webpage of your choice. Click the Backnote extension in your browser bar and select the text of choice in your browser window.
  • Right click on the selected text and hit save to backnotes.
  • Browse to your Backnote page, and there it is! All your notes of one browsing session in one convenient place.
  • You can save and export your backnotes to various formats, such as pdf, doc or html.
