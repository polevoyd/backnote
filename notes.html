How does it work?

Ever wanted an easier way to copy notes on your computer?

We have a solution.

We made it easy for you! Just follow these steps :)

  • Go to https://addons.mozilla.org, search for Backnote and download the extension. Once downloaded, the extension will appear in the top right corner of your browser bar.
  • Go to the webpage of your choice.
  • Right click on the selected text and save to backnotes.
  • Click the Backnote extension in your browser bar.
  • A new tab will pop up to the Backnote page, and there it is! All your notes of one browsing session in one convenient place.

  • You can save and export your backnotes to various formats, such as pdf.
